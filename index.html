How to find weekly, meaningful conversation in State College, PA.

Does this sound familiar?


"Wow, cold day, huh?"

"So... what do you do?"

"Did you see that cat video on Facebook?"


Unfortunately, we had many conversations like this too, until recently.

Now we have chats more like this:


"Where would you go if you could book a one-way ticket to anywhere in the world?"

"What would you tell a 10-year-old version of yourself right now?"

"What's the thing you most want to do? How can you make that happen?"


That's right: intentional, interesting conversation.
