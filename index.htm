How to find weekly, meaningful conversation in State College, PA.

Does this sound familiar?


"Wow, cold day, huh?"

"So... what do you do?"

"Did you see that cat video on Facebook?"


Unfortunantely, we had many conversations like this too, until recently.

Now we have chats more like this:


"Where would you go if you could book a one-way ticket to anywhere in the world?"

"What would you tell a 10-year-old version of yourself right now?"

"What's the thing you most want to do? How can you make that happen?"


That's right: intentional, interesting conversation.


There are people right here in State College that live for this type of talk, and we created Coffeestain so you can have it too.

Every week we'll pair you with a coffee companion and provide a topic to prompt a meaningful interaction. Sometimes, we'll mix it up and host a group cocktail and appetizer hour (or two).

And, for a brief period now, we're accepting new members. Here's how you can snag a spot:
